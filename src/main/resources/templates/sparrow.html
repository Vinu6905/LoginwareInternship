SPARROW

SPARROW (Streamlined Production and Agile Resource Optimization Workflow) is a powerful Manufacturing Operations Management Software developed by Loginware. Designed to streamline manufacturing and service execution from start to finish, SPARROW offers a comprehensive set of features and capabilities to optimize operations, enhance productivity, and ensure high-quality output.

Streamlined Efficiency Takes Flight.

Production Planning

SPARROW enables efficient production planning by providing tools to create and optimize production schedules based on demand, resource availability, and capacity.

Inventory Management

With SPARROW's advanced inventory management features, manufacturers can achieve optimal control over their inventory. The software provides real-time visibility into inventory levels, tracks stock movements,and helps prevent stockouts

Asset Management

SPARROW includes comprehensive asset management capabilities, allowing manufacturers to effectively track and maintain information about their manufacturing equipment, tools, and other assets

Payroll Management

SPARROW simplifies the complex task of payroll management. It provides features to track employee attendance, manage working hours, calculate salaries, and handle other payroll-related processes.

Product Inspection

SPARROW incorporates robust product inspection functionalities to ensure consistent and high-quality output. The software enables the creation of inspection checklists, captures inspection results, generates comprehensive reports.

APQP & PPAP Compliance

SPARROW supports compliance with industry standards such as Advanced Product Quality Planning (APQP) and Production Part Approval Process (PPAP).
