Our Learning Model

Fourth Quadrant Learning : first of its kind.

We have following engagement models.

QUID Model : In House Talent Acquisition


  • Qfour Learning Solutions conducts regular, longer term training programs for Fresh engineers.
  • Our systematic, hands-on training programs transition college graduates to skilled engineers .
  • Qfour Learning Solutions is open to collaborate with hiring organizations by providing skilled engineers trained In house.
  • Skilled engineers can be hired with no cost by Hiring organization.
  • Technologies: Embedded Systems, Linux System Programming, ML and IOT.

QUAD MODEL : On Board Corporate Training






  • Qfour Learning provides domain specific training for experience professionals in a continuous manner for business advantage.
  • Training delivery models:
  • On-demand: Custom training programs
  • Strategic: Long term, life-cycle oriented programs
  • Highly focused on creating business impact with specific competency development with deeper expertise.
  • Combination of various methods (Classroom, Self learning, Hands-on etc.).

Campus ConneQt : On Campus Talent Acquisition


  • Qfour Learning Solutions is working with various academic institutions by taking ownership to create technology impact and interest among Engineering students.
  • The team has trained students from more than 40+ Engineering Colleges across Karnataka.
  • With a very good relationship across various academic institutions, Qfour Learning Solutions will be able to connect young talent across various engineering colleges with the companies and help the companies to conduct smooth campus hiring process.
